About the Conference

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Sustainable Innovation (ICAISI) 2025 aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policymakers to discuss the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in addressing global challenges related to sustainable development. This prestigious conference provides a platform for sharing innovative research, exploring new ideas, and fostering collaborations that can drive forward both technological advancements and environmental sustainability. ICAISI 2025 focuses on a wide range of topics, including but not limited to AI for energy efficiency, climate change mitigation, smart cities, healthcare solutions, and education, all aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

One of the unique aspects of ICAISI 2025 is its emphasis on interdisciplinary research and real-world applications. The conference will feature keynote sessions by world-renowned experts, technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and workshops designed to equip participants with cutting-edge knowledge and skills. Moreover, it will provide opportunities for young researchers and professionals to showcase their work through poster presentations and networking sessions. By fostering discussions on ethical AI practices and equitable technology distribution, ICAISI 2025 aims to ensure that the benefits of AI are accessible to all, leaving no one behind in the pursuit of a sustainable future.

About Suresh Gyan Vihar University

Suresh Gyan Vihar University (SGVU), located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, is a leading private university recognized for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. Established in 2008, SGVU has consistently focused on providing students with a holistic education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. Accredited by NAAC with an 'A' grade, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as engineering, management, science, agriculture, and humanities. Its state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry-relevant curriculum, and dedicated faculty ensure that students are well-equipped to meet global challenges.

A hallmark of SGVU is its emphasis on research and sustainability. The university fosters a culture of inquiry through its advanced research centers, collaborations with leading industries, and participation in global academic partnerships. SGVU’s eco-friendly campus reflects its commitment to sustainable development and innovation. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning and cutting-edge technology, SGVU aims to nurture leaders who can drive positive societal and environmental change, making it a preferred choice for students aspiring to achieve academic and professional success.

Paper Structure

📌 Title & Abstract

Craft a clear and descriptive title for your paper. Write an informative abstract that summarizes the main objectives, methods, results, and conclusions of your research.

🔑 Keywords

Include a list of keywords that accurately represent the content of your paper. These keywords help index your paper for search engines and databases.

📖 Introduction

Provide a comprehensive introduction that outlines the background, significance, and objectives of your research. Clearly state the research questions or hypotheses.

⚙️ Methods

Describe the methodology and techniques used in your study in sufficient detail to allow replication. Include information on data collection, analysis, and any experimental procedures.

📊 Results

Present your findings clearly and concisely. Use tables, figures, and graphs where appropriate to enhance understanding.

💡 Discussion

Interpret your results and discuss their implications. Compare your findings with existing literature and explain any limitations or future directions for research.

📝 Conclusion

Summarize the main findings of your study and restate the importance of your research in the broader context.

📚 References

Cite all sources used in your paper accurately. Follow the citation style specified by the journal or conference guidelines.

✅ Ethical Considerations

Ensure your research complies with ethical standards, including obtaining necessary permissions for data collection and respecting participant confidentiality.

🔍 Proofreading

Proofread your paper carefully for grammar, spelling, and formatting errors. Consider seeking feedback from colleagues or mentors before submission.
